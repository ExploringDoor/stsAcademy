Our Story

Built in Stephenville,
for Stephenville.

Small Town Sports Academy opened its doors in 2023, founded and run by Amanda Philips with a simple idea: small-town kids deserve big-league coaching without leaving home. What started as a place to get good reps in has grown into a year-round home for ballplayers of every age.

We're the home of the Sox — our competitive baseball and softball program — and we're proud to be family-run and small-town to the core. Around here, coaches know your kid by name, follow their swing season to season, and treat every player like one of our own.

We're also the sister organization to Small Town Select Tournaments, the tournament side of the same family. That connection gives our players a path to real competition — but make no mistake, the academy is where it all starts, one rep at a time.
