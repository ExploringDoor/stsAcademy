How it works

The short version — and where to go when you're ready to sign up.

How do team memberships work?

Team memberships are organized by age group, so your player trains and competes alongside teammates at their level. Each one is offered as a month-to-month plan or a single full-season package — pick whichever fits your family's budget and calendar.

Can non-team families use the facility?

Absolutely. The Public Family Membership gives non-Sox families full access to our cages, turf, and open training time — no team commitment required.

How do I enroll?

Every plan is enrolled and paid for on our booking page. Browse the live age-group options, choose monthly or full-season, and you're set in a couple of taps — enroll on our booking page.
